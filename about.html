Our Inspiration

Our inspiration comes from Thailand, where the founder discovered sportswear that delivered exceptional quality at a reasonable price. After years of personal experience wearing these products, it became clear that outstanding performance doesn't always require a premium price.

One special piece of sportswear has remained with the founder since the 24th SEA Games held in Nakhon Ratchasima, Thailand, in 2007. Even after many years, it is still in excellent condition and continues to be worn casually today. That lasting quality inspired the vision behind CHAYNOVA.

Our Vision

CHAYNOVA is more than a sportswear brand.

We want to encourage people of all ages to live active, meaningful, and enjoyable lives. Whether it's playing sports, listening to music, enjoying computer games, or continuing to learn new skills, we believe every stage of life offers opportunities to grow.

We believe that confidence often begins with taking the first step. Sometimes that first step is as simple as putting on clothing that makes you feel comfortable, capable, and ready to move.

For us, sportswear is not just something you wear. It can become a tool that helps you build confidence, step outside your comfort zone, and discover new possibilities.

Our Promise

We carefully select products that we would proudly wear ourselves. Our goal is to offer sportswear that provides excellent quality, lasting durability, comfort, and great value.

As CHAYNOVA grows, we hope to build more than a store. We hope to build a community that inspires people to move, learn, play, and enjoy life together.

Thank you for being part of the CHAYNOVA journey.
