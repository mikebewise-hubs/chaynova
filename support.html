HOW CAN WE HELP?

Welcome to CHAYNOVA Support. This page will grow as we learn what questions and order issues our customers need help with.

Online Order Help

Check your confirmation email.
Make sure the order number, items, shipping address, and payment information are correct.

Check your tracking information.
Shipping updates may take some time to appear after your order is prepared or transferred to the delivery carrier.

Check your spam or junk folder.
Order confirmations, payment notices, and shipping updates may sometimes be filtered by your email provider.

Do not place the same order again immediately.
First check your email and bank account to confirm whether the original payment or order was completed.

When contacting support, please include your name, order number, the email address used for the order, and a short description of the issue. Do not send your full credit card number or password. Email us at support@chaynova.com.

More questions and answers will be added here soon.
